The Artist Behind The Studio

Hi, I'm Selenne —
welcome to my chair.

What started as a genuine obsession with the craft has grown into a full lash studio built on one idea: your lashes should feel like you, only rested and a little more awake. Every appointment starts with a real conversation about your eye shape, your routine, and how low-maintenance or dramatic you want to go.

No two sets leave my chair looking the same, because no two clients are the same. That's the whole point.

"I map every set by hand before I ever pick up the tweezers — that's the difference between lashes that just look good in the mirror, and lashes that look good on you."
— Selenne
